How does AddJob work?

AddJob is an online platform where it connects those who offers service and those who need it. Users can choose whether they want to be an "Employee" or "Employer". As the name suggests, Employees are those who are offering services and Employers are those who hire the employees.

In AddJob, Employers can choose from a wide variety of services, mostly house services, and who to hire. They may base their choices on the distance or the reviews/rating of the previous employers. Upon choosing, the employer can contact the said employee through the chat system here in AddJob. The two can then set up the schedule that suits them the most. Payment can be done through credit card.

For more inquiries, you can contact us through our Facebook page (AddJob), email (AddJob@gmail.com), twitter (@addjob) or instagram (@addjob).
